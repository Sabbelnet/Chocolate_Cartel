Gamerules

The target of the Game is to make as much Capital as you can. 
Use it to upgrade your buildings to be able to produce sugar, milk, cacao and chocolate.
To be able to upgrade your buildings past level 1 you need to upgrade your laboratory first.
Your buildings will start to produce ressources. 
Those ressources will be send to your warehouse, watch out that you dont overfill it!
You can sell or buy additional ones from the Shop Button.
With the highscore Button you can view the Highscore of your Game.
